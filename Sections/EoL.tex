\begin{frame}{Others challenges and perspectives}
    \begin{block}{Key processing constraints for mechanically rPE}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Tolerance to contaminants depends on the process (thin films less forgiving).
            \item Common contaminants: paper fibre, other polymers, rubber.
            \item Branched gels in rPE limit stretchability.
            \item Optical quality: many rPE grades are greyish vs.\ virgin PE.
            \item regulation for food grade. 
        \end{itemize}
    \end{block}
\end{frame}
